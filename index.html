How It Works

What's Under The Hood?

  • Step 1

    Public Record

    Building permits, date of construction, square footage, type of heat/cooling systems, fuel type, and more data points are fed into our software to produce a profile of the home’s most likely energy performance level.

  • Step 2

    Energy Modeling

    We produce an estimated energy rating that is comparable to the nationally recognized energy rating standard (the HERS rating), then give it a color scheme so that you understand how this compares with other homes.

  • Step 3

    Local Pricing

    The estimated energy rating is accompanied by an estimated annual energy cost, adjusted for the local utility rates in the home’s service territory.
